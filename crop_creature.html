Make a Crop Creature

Build a silly creature out of real fruits & veggies — then watch it come to life! 🥒🐛

  • 1
    Pick a body. A big zucchini, cucumber or potato makes a great body.
  • 2
    Add legs & ears. Push toothpicks into small veggies (cherry tomatoes, beans, pepper bits) and attach them.
  • 3
    Shape your creature. A spiky dino, a doggo, a hedgehog — anything you imagine!
  • 4
    Snap a photo on a plain background — the app adds eyes, a nose & a mouth and brings it to life! ✨

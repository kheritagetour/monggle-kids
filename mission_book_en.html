DAY 8 · KOREA SPECIALKorean culture · Creativity

The Dancing Mask (Talchum 탈춤)

Moong found an old wooden mask in a Korean village. When the drums played — dung, dung! — the mask began to smile… and dance! “Every Korean mask has its own face and feeling. What would YOUR mask feel?”

🟢 Did you know? · Korea • Korean masks are called Tal (탈); the Hahoe masks are 800+ years old.
• Talchum (탈춤), the Korean mask dance, is on the UNESCO list!
• Hangeul (한글) is the Korean alphabet · Hanbok (한복) is the colorful traditional dress.
🟣 Culture Note · Feelings in motionEach mask shows one big feeling — a wide laugh, a sly grin, a kind smile. The dance turns feelings into movement — just like Mongle creatures coming to life!

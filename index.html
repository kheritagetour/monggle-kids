Chapter 01 — The Story

When the Grumble stole all the feelings…

One gray morning, a sneaky gloom monster zoomed into Monggle Town and locked everyone's feelings away. Colors faded. Friends went quiet. Hugs stopped.

But feelings are stronger than gloom — and every great rescue needs a brave new Heart Hero.
